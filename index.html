Our Project - Insight App

Mobile devices can be found in the hands of every person from every walk of life, and often from a very young age. While the heavy use of technology and some apps and social platforms have been studied to be harmful for young developing minds, the technology can also be applied to discover and improve the welfare and performance of young individuals. This project concept is to develop an app for school students to provide feedback that can be collected by teachers and the school for addressing issues with students’ social, psychological, and physical wellbeing; and in turn, improve learning ability and general health.

Insight is a school student survey mobile application. Our aim is to deliver a product that will improve the health and wellbeing of school children around the country through intuitive mobile applications.
